One Arrested because of attacking with khukuri.

One person has been arrested for attacking another person with a khukuri in a restaurant in Nawalparasi Bardghat Municipality-4 On Tuesday night, two people were attacked among whom one died and the other is in critical condition. According to DSP Bhojraj Pandey, 50-year-old Gyanendra Bahadur Rana of Bardghat Municipality-2 was arrested for his involvement in the incident. Police said that Rana, who reached the restaurant at around 9 o'clock on Tuesday night, attacked the owner of the shop, 42-year-old Bishnu Kumari Kauchha, and 46-year-old Dilip Kumar Pun, who was sitting in the room. Pun, who was seriously injured, died on Tuesday night during treatment. The deceased was a resident of Badigad Rural Municipality-4, Baglung. Bishnu, who is undergoing treatment at Bhairawa Medical College, and is in critical condition, is also from Baglung's Badigad. Police said that for the past 4-5 years, Bishnu has been running a restaurant in Bardghat alone. DSP Pandey said that Rana, who ran away after the attack, was arrested from his own house. The incident is being investigated.
Games of the week. PUBG PUBG Mobile[a] (Chinese: 和平精英; pinyin: Hé Píng Jīng Yīng) is a free-to-play battle royale video game developed by LightSpeed & Quantum Studio, a division of Tencent Games. It is a mobile game adaptation of PUBG: Battlegrounds. It was initially released for Android and iOS on 19 March 2018. It was published by multiple publishers in different regions, including Krafton, Tencent, and VNG Games.[1][2] By May 2022, PUBG Mobile had accumulated over a billion downloads while grossing over $8.42 billion, making it the fourth highest-grossing mobile game.[3][4] It is also the most-played mobile video game of all time. In 2021, the game spawned an Indian version, Battlegrounds Mobile India, and a separate game taking place in the PUBG Universe, New State Mobile.
